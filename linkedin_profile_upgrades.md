2. About Section Rewrite -
"Here is my about section: [paste]. My audience is [who you want to attract]. The main thing I want them to know is [your core value]. Rewrite it. First two lines hook into clicking see more. What I do, who I help, one result. End with a CTA. Under 2000 characters. First person."
Use Claude

Use ChatGPT or Gemini
